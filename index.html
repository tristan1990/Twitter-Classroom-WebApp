Twitter-Classroom-Web App

Really small little app, perfect for use in the classroom. Downloads a PDF of your (fake) tweet... students love it.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

What is this about.

This is a little app I created for students to use in the classroom. Ideally it is used at the end of a lesson so that students can leave a 'tweet' about what they have learned. I'd love to collaborate with other educators on this, I think it could be really interesting to hear peoples ideas.
